The development of gaze following in monolingual and bilingual infants: A multi-laboratory study
We used a gaze-following paradigm developed by Senju and Csibra (Current Biology, 18, 2008, 668) to test a total of 93 6- to 9-month-old and 229 12- to 15-month-old monolingual and bilingual infants, in 11 laboratories located in 8 countries.

Impact of language dominance on phonetic transfer in Cantonese–English bilingual language switching
Using a language-switching paradigm, this study investigated differences in phonetic transfer of Cantonese–English bilingual adults with various language dominance profiles (Cantonese-dominant, English-dominant, and balanced bilinguals).
Rachel K.Y. Tsui, Xiuli Tong, Chan Siu Ki Chan
